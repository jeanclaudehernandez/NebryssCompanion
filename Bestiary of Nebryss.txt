### **Bestiary of Nebryss**  
**Power Rating (PR) Formula**  
- **PR** = `(Wounds × 2.2) + [(6 − Save) × 7] + Weapon Threat + Ability Score + (Movement × 4) + (APL × 6)`  
  - **Wounds**: Wound count × 2.2.  
  - **Save Modifier**: Subtract the Save value from 6, then multiply by 7.  
  - **Weapon Threat**: (Total Attacks × Average Damage) + Special Rule value.  
  - **Ability Score**: +7 per impactful ability.  
  - **Movement**: Movement value × 4.  
  - **APL**: APL value × 6.
