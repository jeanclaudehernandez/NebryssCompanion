### **The Imperium of Man**

#### **Inquisitor Veyra Mortis – Ordo Hereticus**
- **Mission**: Purge all knowledge of the mist’s true nature and eliminate heretical elements.
- **Methods**: Works with Adeptus Sororitas and Imperial Navy forces; employs infiltrators and sanctioned purges.
- **Personality**: Fanatical, brutal, and uncompromising.
- **Location**: Primarily stationed at **Fortress Sanctus**, where she oversees purges and enforces Imperial doctrine.

#### **Inquisitor Alaric Dresdan – Ordo Malleus**
- **Mission**: Harness the mist’s properties for the Imperium’s benefit.
- **Methods**: Works with radical Magi of the Adeptus Mechanicus and sanctioned psykers; conducts clandestine experiments.
- **Personality**: Pragmatic, methodical, and controversial.
- **Location**: Primarily stationed at **Stormwatch**, where he studies mist patterns and coordinates with Imperial forces.

#### **Captain Marcus Valen – White Consuls**
- **Role**: Diplomatic envoy and military advisor to the Imperium’s forces on Nebryss.
- **Mission**: Ensure the stability of Imperial control while fostering alliances with local factions, particularly the Gilded Accord, to secure mist trade routes.
- **Wargear**:
  - **The Lance of Sabatine**: A golden power fist engraved with the emblem of a lancer on horseback, symbolizing the White Consuls’ commitment to swift and decisive action.
  - **Mistforged Plasma Gun**: A custom plasma gun optimized for combat in dense mist, featuring enhanced cooling systems and a targeting array that pierces through the mist’s distortions.
- **Personality**: Diplomatic, honorable, and compassionate, with a focus on strategic thinking and alliance-building.
- **Location**: Stationed at **Fortress Sanctus**, but frequently travels to **Stormwatch** to assist Inquisitor Alaric Dresdan with mist-related operations.

### **The Gilded Accord**

#### **Nebryssian Reserve Director Thalos Grenn**
- **Role**: The head of the Nebryssian Reserve, Thalos oversees the minting and distribution of Mistral coins and manages the Accord’s digital financial systems.
- **Personality**: Meticulous, cautious, and highly intelligent. He is a master of finance but struggles with interpersonal relationships.
- **Location**: **Luminos**.

#### **Kael "Stormchaser" Draven**
- **Role**: A legendary navigator and one of the most skilled members of the Fogbound Cartographers. Known for his daring rescues and ability to deliver VIPs through the most perilous mist zones.
- **Location**: Operates from **Zephyria** and other key locations along mist trade routes.

---

### **The Nebryssian Liberation Republic**

#### **Commander Elara Voss**
- **Role**: The charismatic leader of the NLR, known for her tactical brilliance and unwavering dedication to Nebryss’s independence.
- **Personality**: Inspiring, resourceful, and fiercely protective of her people.
- **Location**: Primarily stationed at the **Veiled Citadel** in **Misthaven**.

#### **Mistweaver Kaelen Draymoor**
- **Role**: A revered Mistweaver who has survived multiple rituals, earning him the title “The Unbroken.”
- **Personality**: Wise, enigmatic, and deeply spiritual.
- **Location**: Resides in the **Mistweaver’s Grove**, where he trains new Mistweavers and conducts rituals.

---

### **The Crimson Corsairs**

#### **Lady Seraphine "The Siren" Voss**
- **Reputation**: A former Rogue Trader turned pirate, Seraphine uses her charm and cunning to manipulate her enemies. She is known for seducing her targets into lowering their guard before striking.
- **Backstory**: Accused of heresy by the Inquisition, she fled to Nebryss to evade persecution and rebuild her power.
- **Fleet Size**: 4 ships (small and stealthy).
- **Flagship**: **The Crimson Siren** – A luxurious but deadly vessel disguised as a trading ship, equipped with hidden weaponry and a cloaking device.
- **Tactics**: Relies on deception, often posing as a merchant or ally before revealing her true intentions and attacking.
- **Motivations**: Seeks to rebuild her power and wealth while evading her relatives and the Inquisition.

#### **Garrick "The Butcher" Malrik**
- **Reputation**: A brutal and bloodthirsty pirate who leaves no survivors. Garrick is feared for his savagery and his fleet’s relentless pursuit of prey.
- **Fleet Size**: 2 ships (one medium-sized raider and one large flagship).
- **Flagship**: **The Bloodied Cutlass** – A massive, heavily armored raider with reinforced hulls and a ramming prow designed to tear through enemy ships.
- **Tactics**: Prefers direct assaults, using his flagship’s surprising speed and firepower to overwhelm his targets.
- **Motivations**: Thrives on violence and plunder, seeking to amass wealth and infamy through sheer brutality.
