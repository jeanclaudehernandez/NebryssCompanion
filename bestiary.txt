### **Bestiary of Nebryss**  
**Revised Power Rating (PR) Formula**  
**PR** = `(Wounds × 2.2) + [(6 − Save) × 7] + Weapon Threat + Ability Score + (Movement × 4) + (APL × 6)`  
- **Weapon Threat**: `(Attacks × min Damage) + Special Rules + (7 − WS)` highest value of all weapons.  
  - **Special Rules**: Positive (e.g., *Lethal 5+*) = **+2**, Negative (e.g., *Heavy*) = **−1**.  
- **Ability Score**: **+7 per impactful ability**.
